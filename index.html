Got questions?

Frequently asked questions

Do I need to know how to code to use FormFlow?
No coding required. You build your form in the visual editor, then copy a single <script> tag into your site. Works with Webflow, WordPress, Framer, Squarespace, or raw HTML.
How does the Stripe integration work?
Connect your Stripe account in one click from the FormFlow dashboard. Payment fields are automatically added to your form and all transactions go directly to your Stripe account — FormFlow never touches your money. Supports one-time payments and recurring subscriptions.
What does "WCAG 2.1 AA compliant" actually mean for my forms?
Every form you create is usable by people with disabilities — screen readers, keyboard-only navigation, high-contrast displays. Every field has a proper label, error messages are announced automatically, focus is always visible, and color is never the only way information is conveyed. For healthcare, government, and education sectors this is also a legal requirement.
Will FormFlow slow down my website?
No. The embed script loads asynchronously so it never blocks your page render. There is zero Cumulative Layout Shift (CLS) because the form container reserves its space before the script runs. FormFlow has no impact on your Lighthouse score or Core Web Vitals.
Can I cancel or switch plans at any time?
Yes — upgrade, downgrade, or cancel from your account settings at any time. You keep access until the end of your billing period, and we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans, no questions asked.
Is my data and my customers' data secure?
All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). Card details are handled entirely by Stripe — we never store payment information on our servers. FormFlow is GDPR-compliant and SOC 2 Type II audited.
